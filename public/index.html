The Mission


" With “I” and “me” representing U in the next couple of paragraphs – here is what this book intends to bring to surface... "


When I am truly selfish, it is about focusing on being the best I can be, because I deserve what comes from being my best. Being lovingly selfish is about giving love to others from the love that is within me, for the greatness that I feel on the inside. When I am truly selfish; I will forgive who has hurt me throughout life, not for them or because they deserve forgiveness, but because I’m meant to live in peace. This type of selfishness is about being my own biggest fan because I deserve to feel special. Being selfish is really about the satisfaction I receive within for helping someone and, not for the gratitude I may expect in return. When I am genuinely selfish, I become someone that I can look up to and someone that I can respect. This kind of selfishness is about living a life that is positive and favorable, being that, I am the loving spirit that I am, and, I am someone I also appreciate. This is a way of living out an existence full of excellence by doing what brings me greatness to my life. When I am truly being selfish, I am doing what is right for my life because it benefits me personally to grow, and not because someone has told me, how I should live.
